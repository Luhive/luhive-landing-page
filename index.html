About Luhive

The Problem

Communities today are fragmented. Event management happens on Google Forms or Luma. Announcements are scattered across Telegram, WhatsApp, and Discord. Feedback gets lost in the noise.

It's time for a change.

Our Solution

Luhive brings everything together — events, communication, feedback, and community engagement — all in one beautifully designed platform.

Verified communities that want real connections and zero chaos. From tech communities to educational organizations.

Our Vision

Starting in Azerbaijan, scaling globally. We're building a platform that empowers communities worldwide to connect, engage, and thrive together.
