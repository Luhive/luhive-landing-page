About Luhive

The Future of Community Management

We're building the ultimate community platform for verified organizations. Starting in Azerbaijan, scaling globally.

The Problem

Communities today are fragmented. Event management happens on Google Forms. Announcements are scattered across Telegram, WhatsApp, and Discord. Feedback gets lost in the noise.

It's time for a change.

Our Solution

Luhive brings everything together — events, communication, feedback, and community engagement — all in one beautifully designed platform.

Who We Serve

Verified communities that want real connections and zero chaos. From tech communities to educational organizations.

Our Vision

Starting in Azerbaijan, scaling globally. We're building a platform that empowers communities worldwide to connect, engage, and thrive together. Our mission is to break down barriers, foster meaningful connections, and create spaces where every community can flourish—no matter where they are.

Key Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

Unified Event Management

Create, manage, and track events effortlessly. No more Google Forms. Just one powerful system that works.

Centralized Communication

Say goodbye to scattered chats. All your community conversations, announcements, and updates in one place.

Smart Feedback System

Collect, organize, and act on community feedback. Never lose a suggestion or request again.

Verified Communities

Join authentic, verified communities. Build trust and credibility with official community badges.

Analytics & Insights

Understand your community better with powerful analytics. Track engagement, growth, and member activity.

Privacy & Security

Your community's data is safe with us. Enterprise-grade security meets user-friendly design.
